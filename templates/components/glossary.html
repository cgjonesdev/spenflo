Cashburn
Cashburn is the amount of money you are spending during a pay period. The amount of money is taken from the Budget's spending category for a given pay period. As the pay period progresses and money is spent, the spent amount is tracked against the time that's gone by. If you are spending more money than you should be, then you would be ovespending.
Stats
Stats are a way of measuring your progress. All Stats are important, but the most important is your Spenflo Score. This gives an overall rating of your financial health and takes all the other Stats figures into consideration.
Budget
Your Budget is a breakdown of 3 simple categories: Bills, Savings, and Spending, specfic to the pay period you are in. No need for envelopes or pie charts trying to figure out how much money to put in each category. The Budget tells you how much to set aside for bills each paycheck, how much you should save, and what you can spend each pay period without getting into trouble.
